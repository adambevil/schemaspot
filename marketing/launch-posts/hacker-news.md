I built this because local businesses are missing out on one of the easiest SEO improvements — structured data. Schema.org is well-documented but manually writing JSON-LD is tedious and error-prone.

This tool generates valid, Google-compliant markup for 10 business types. No signup, no credit card, runs entirely client-side (your data never leaves your browser).

Happy to answer questions about the implementation or Schema.org in general!
