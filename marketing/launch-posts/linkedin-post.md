Local businesses are losing organic traffic every day for one simple reason: they don't have structured data on their websites.

Google literally recommends adding JSON-LD schema markup. It's one of the few SEO improvements that Google openly endorses. And it directly impacts how businesses appear in search results — rich snippets, knowledge panels, and local pack rankings.

The problem? Generating valid JSON-LD requires either a developer, an expensive SEO tool, or hours of reading Google's documentation.

So I built SchemaSpot.

It's a free, no-signup tool that generates valid JSON-LD for local businesses in under 60 seconds. You pick your business type, fill in a simple form, and copy the markup.

Supports 10 business types: restaurants, medical clinics, legal services, home contractors, retail, auto repair, beauty salons, fitness centers, professional services, and hotels.

Why I built it free: Most local businesses can't afford $500+ monthly SEO retainers. This gives them one powerful, actionable improvement they can implement themselves today.

Try it here: https://schemaspot.netlify.app/
