Free JSON-LD schema generator for local businesses

Topic:
SEO, Productivity, Web Design, Developer Tools

Description:
SchemaSpot is a free, no-signup tool that generates valid JSON-LD structured data for local businesses. Pick your business type, fill in a simple form, and get clean schema markup you can paste into any site — no coding required.

Supports restaurants, medical clinics, legal services, home contractors, retail stores, auto repair, beauty salons, fitness centers, professional services, and hotels. Built for local businesses that need better Google visibility but don't have the budget for expensive SEO tools.
