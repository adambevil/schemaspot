The easiest SEO win for local businesses is also the most ignored: schema markup.

A restaurant with proper schema can show:
- Star ratings in search
- Menu items
- Price range
- Open hours

A dentist can show:
- Services offered
- Appointment booking
- Reviews

All of this requires ZERO paid ads.

It just needs valid JSON-LD on your site.
