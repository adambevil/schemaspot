## Listing Details

**Product Name:** SchemaSpot
**Tagline:** Free schema markup generator for local businesses
**URL:** https://schemaspot.netlify.app/
**Category:** Web App, SEO, Developer Tools, Small Business

**Twitter:** @adambevil

**Description:**
Generate valid JSON-LD schema markup for any local business in seconds. No sign-up, no paywall, no developer required.

SchemaSpot supports 20+ schema types including LocalBusiness, FAQ, Article, Product, Event, MedicalBusiness, and more. Just pick your business type, fill in your details, and get production-ready markup that passes Google's Rich Results Test.

**Key Features (for PH listing):**
- 20+ business types with tailored schema generation
- Live Google search result preview
- Copy-paste JSON-LD output
- Platform-specific install instructions (WordPress, Wix, Shopify, Squarespace)
- Built-in validation
- 100% free, no sign-up required
- Open source

## Maker Comment

"Hey Product Hunters! I'm Adam, and I built SchemaSpot after noticing how many local business websites miss out on Rich Snippets in Google simply because they don't have structured data implemented.

The problem: Schema markup requires technical knowledge that most small business owners don't have, and SEO agencies charge $100-500+ for basic implementation.

The solution: A dead-simple, free tool that generates valid JSON-LD schema markup in under 60 seconds. No sign-up, no paywall. I built it with Next.js and it's open source on GitHub.
