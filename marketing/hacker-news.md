## Comments (to post after submission)

### Comment 1 (Immediately after posting)
I built this after noticing that most local business websites either don't have structured data or have broken/schema.org-compliant JSON-LD. Rich snippets in Google (ratings, hours, FAQ answers etc.) can increase CTR by 30%+, but implementing schema markup correctly requires technical knowledge that most small business owners don't have.

SchemaSpot is a zero-signup, zero-paywall tool that generates valid JSON-LD for 20+ schema types (LocalBusiness, FAQ, Article, Product, etc.). Just pick your business type, fill in your details, and get production-ready markup. Built with Next.js, open source.

Happy to answer questions!

### Comment 2 (If asked about implementation)
The tool validates generated markup against schema.org standards and produces JSON-LD that passes Google's Rich Results Test. It handles all the tricky parts like nested types (PostalAddress, OpeningHoursSpecification, GeoCoordinates), proper URL formatting, and escaping special characters.

It also includes platform-specific install instructions — WordPress (via header injection), Wix, Shopify, Squarespace, or manual insertion.

### Comment 3 (If asked about monetization)
It's 100% free right now. I'm using this to validate whether there's actual demand for this kind of tool. If people use it, I might add batch processing and monitoring features later, but the core generator will always be free. No tracking, no email collection, no paywalls.
